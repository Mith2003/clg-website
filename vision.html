VISION AND MISSION



VISION


To be knowledge hub of providing quality technical education and promoting research for building up of our nation and its contribution for the betterment of humanity.


MISSION


  1. To make the best use of state-of-the-art infrastructure to ensure quality technical education.

  2. To develop industrial collaborations to promote innovation and research capabilities.

  3. To inculcate values and ethics to serve humanity
